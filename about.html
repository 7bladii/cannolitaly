Our Story

From Messina to Los Angeles

Our story begins in Messina (Taormina), where my family, who own a bar, perfected their own cannoli recipe over generations. It's a recipe built on love, patience, and the finest local ingredients.

Today, we bring that same tradition to your table in Los Angeles. Every cannoli is hand-filled just moments before it reaches you, ensuring a crispy shell and a rich, creamy filling.

This is not just a dessert. This is the real taste of Sicily.
